1. What OSPOS Is

OSPOS is a mobile point-of-sale application designed for small businesses. The Service allows you to build a menu, process transactions, track sales, and manage your business from your phone.

OSPOS is available in two tiers:

Free Tier. Cash-only point of sale with menu management, sales tracking, daily summaries, and local CSV export. No account required. No internet connection required. All data is stored locally on your device.

Paid Tier. Everything in the Free Tier, plus the ability to accept card payments, send SMS and email receipts, access date-range reporting, and sync data to our servers. The Paid Tier requires an OSPOS account and a connected Stripe account.
