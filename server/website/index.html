Why OSPOS

The POS that doesn't
nickel-and-dime you.

Every other POS charges you monthly, plus per-transaction fees, plus hardware costs, plus add-on fees. We charge nothing for cash and 1% for cards. That's it.

$0

Free forever, actually.

Cash-only POS with menu builder, order tracking, daily summaries, and CSV export. No account needed. No internet required. Works completely offline. Zero dollars, zero catches.

+1%

Cards? Just one percent on top.

Accept tap, chip, and swipe payments through Stripe. Stripe charges its standard 2.7% + 5¢ per in-person transaction — and OSPOS adds a separate 1% on top. That's the whole deal. No monthly fees, no minimums, no hidden charges.

Compare: Square 2.6% + 10¢ · Toast $69/mo + fees · Clover $14.95/mo + fees
60s

Set up in sixty seconds.

Download, build your menu, start selling. Card payments take five minutes to enable with Stripe. No hardware required — Tap to Pay on iPhone works right out of the box.

Your data stays yours.

All data stored locally on your device. We never see your sales data unless you opt into cloud sync. No card numbers ever touch our servers. Open source — audit the code yourself.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Menu Builder

Items with stickers, categories, and modifier groups. Customers customize, you ring fast.

Modifier Groups

Choose-your-milk, add-toppings, no-onions. Required vs optional, single vs multi, defaults that pre-fill the customize sheet.

Sold-Out Toggle

86 an item or modifier mid-shift with one tap. Hides from the order grid without deleting. Toggle back when you restock.

Order Discounts

Percent off or dollar off, with an optional reason like “Happy hour” or “Birthday comp.” Math handles taxable items correctly.

QSR Mode

Optional category filter strip above the order grid for menus with 20+ items. Coffee, sandwiches, tacos — filter and ring fast.

Tap to Pay

Accept contactless cards with just your iPhone. No reader needed. Uses Apple's built-in NFC.

Sales Tracking

Daily summaries, date-range reports, and CSV export. Know exactly how your business is doing.

Offline Mode

Cash register works with zero internet. Sell anywhere — no WiFi, no cellular, no problem.

Email Receipts

Email receipts to your customers. Clean, branded, professional.

Dispute Protection

Get notified of chargebacks and submit evidence directly from the app.

Live in v1.1

Modifiers, shipped

Build modifier groups per item — milk, size, add-ons — with required or optional choices, single or multi-select, and upcharges. A customize sheet pops up the moment you ring it in, and any cart line stays editable.

Live in v1.1

QSR mode

Flip on a category strip above the grid so big menus filter by Coffee, Sandwiches, or Pastries in a tap. It stays hidden for simple menus — no clutter for the coffee cart.

Live in v1.1

86 it in a tap

Sold out? Hide an item from the order grid the second you run out, then flip it back when you restock. No deleting, no rebuilding your menu. Order-level discounts and comps land too.

Live in v1.1

193 stickers

The sticker library jumps from 46 to 193 icons across Food, Drinks, Retail, and Service, with a full-screen picker, search, and category tabs so every item on your grid is recognizable.

June 30: repo flips public at github.com/pzapzap/ospos under AGPL-3.0. Audit it, fork it, self-host it. Hosted version stays on the App Store at 1% on cards.
