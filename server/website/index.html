What OSPOS does

Build your menu

Add items with names, prices, and photos. Edit or remove anytime.

Take orders fast

Tap items to build an order. Haptic feedback on every tap. Designed for speed.

Accept payments

Cash or card. Tap to Pay on iPhone — no extra hardware needed.

See your sales

Daily summary, transaction history, CSV export. All your data on your phone.

Send receipts

Text, email, or print to a Bluetooth thermal printer.

Works offline

Cash transactions work without internet. Card payments sync when you're back online.

Simple pricing

No monthly fees. No hidden costs.
