Delete Your Account

Effective Date: July 19, 2026 · Last Updated: July 19, 2026

You can delete your OSPOS account and all associated server-stored data at any time. This page describes both the in-app method (recommended) and the email fallback for users who no longer have the app installed.

Delete from within the app

This is the fastest and most secure method. Deletion is confirmed with your device biometric.

  1. Open OSPOS on your device.
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Scroll to the Account section and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode.

Your account and server-stored data are marked for deletion immediately. See the retention schedule below.

Deletion by email

If you have already uninstalled the app, or you cannot access it for any reason, request deletion by email.

Send a message from the email address associated with your OSPOS account to phil@ospos.app with the subject line “Account Deletion Request.” We process such requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Email address, sign-in provider ID, password hashRetained 90 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
Business name, business settingsRetained 90 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
Order and transaction history (server-stored)Retained 90 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
Receipt-recipient email or phone numbersDeleted with the associated order
Push notification tokenDeleted immediately
Anonymized aggregate statisticsRetained (no personal identifiers)

What is NOT deleted

  • Data stored locally on your device. Delete the app to remove local data — OSPOS uses SQLite in the app's private container, which the operating system deletes with the app.
  • Records held by Stripe. Card payments are processed by Stripe. Stripe retains transaction records under its own policy — see Stripe's Privacy Policy. Deleting your OSPOS account does not delete your Stripe account or their transaction records.
  • Records we are legally required to retain. Certain financial records may be retained to satisfy tax, fraud, or dispute-related obligations.

Recovery window

During the 90-day retention window, we can restore your account and associated data on request if you change your mind. After 90 days, deletion is permanent and irreversible. Contact phil@ospos.app during that window if you want to recover a deleted account.
