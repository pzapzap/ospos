## Full Description

Free point of sale for your phone. Take orders, accept card payments, see your sales. No monthly fees. Open source.

OSPOS turns any smartphone into a cash register. Add your menu items, tap to build orders, and charge your customers — cash or card.

**Free tier (no account needed):**
- Build your menu
- Take orders with one tap
- Accept cash payments
- View daily sales summary
- Export transactions as CSV
- Weekly automatic backups
- Works completely offline

**Card payments (1% per transaction):**
- Accept card payments via Tap to Pay on iPhone or Bluetooth reader
- Stripe Connect for secure payment processing
- Test mode to practice before going live
- Cloud backup of all transactions
- Refund management
- Dispute handling with evidence upload
- SMS and email receipts

**Built for speed:**
Every tap gives haptic feedback. The charge button has a 5-state indicator. The order screen is designed to be used while serving customers in a line. Function over form.

**Open source:**
OSPOS is MIT licensed. View the code, report issues, or contribute at github.com/ospos/ospos.

## Review Compliance Notes

**In-App Purchases:**
OSPOS does not use iOS/Android IAP for payment processing. The 1% fee is charged by Stripe on physical goods/services transactions processed through the app, which is exempt from IAP requirements per Apple's App Store Review Guidelines 3.1.3(e) and Google Play's Payments Policy for physical goods.

**Bluetooth:**
Used for connecting to Stripe card readers (M2 Bluetooth reader) and ESC/POS thermal receipt printers.

**Camera:**
Used for taking photos of menu items and capturing dispute evidence images.

**Location:**
Used by Stripe Terminal SDK for reader discovery (required by Stripe).

**NFC:**
Used for Tap to Pay on iPhone contactless payments via Stripe Terminal.
